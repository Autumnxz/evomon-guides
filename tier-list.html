Battle & team building

Evomon tier list — who's actually worth training.

Ranked by real combat value: boss performance, tower consistency, and how well a line scales once evolved — not just base-form stats.

Read this before you commit resources: community tier lists disagree a lot on Evomon, mostly because base forms and fully-evolved lines rank very differently. A monster that looks weak on day one (Bluebird, Gulpfish) can become a top pick once evolved (Vulcest, Mirefish). This list ranks by full evolution line potential — treat it as a starting point for where to invest EXP Fruit and evolution materials, not gospel.

Reading the list

What actually earns a spot in S-tier

Type advantage first

The single biggest factor — is this the strongest option in its type? That beats raw stats almost every time.

Raw damage

Turns are limited, so consistent hard-hitting attacks matter more than flashy but unreliable kits.

Speed and initiative

Acting first in a turn-based fight often decides the outcome before damage numbers even matter.

Team role

Every good 5-slot team needs pressure, control, sustain, and a closer — not six copies of the same job.
